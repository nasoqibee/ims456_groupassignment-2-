About Us

Pata Timor Foodie Hunter is a food blog that is written by a group of friends who absolutely passionate about food and want to share the amazing dishes at best restaurants/cafes we’ve discovered throughout Malaysia in Pata Timor!
Whether you’re a seasoned food lover or someone curious about a cuisine at cafe that located at Pata Timor, we hope to inspire you to explore new flavors and embark on your own food adventures. Join us as we uncover the delicious secrets of variety of food and celebrate the joy it brings! 🤍

Review

Reezqi Singgang Patin that are located at the city of Kuantan has a nice and strategic location that attract customers to come here. Eventhough it is homemade, Patin tempoyak are so tasty and the taste are almost the same like Temerloh. We have been here for a several times to fulfill our cravings! The taste of spicy, sour, salty are just a perfect taste for us. Definitely highly recommended restaurant for people that loves to eat Temerloh cuisine.

Our Review
Customer

Jolly Good Cafe at Pantai Batu Buruk is a great place for breakfast. They have a wide variety of food to choose from, so everyone can find something they like. The ingredients are fresh, and the food is made with care. The cafe has a nice, relaxed atmosphere that makes it a comfortable place to eat. If you're looking for a cozy spot with good food for breakfast, this cafe is a good choice.

Our Review
Customer

Aus Mee Celup is the BEST Mee Celup in town of Kota Bharu. Good service with a lively atmosphere. I came here twice already and still like it. Locking also tasty. Free flow of ice water and comdiments. Price is totally reasonable. Its just a bit hot and parking limited but the food was great and affordable. The best thing on the menu would be the mango float and dragon float!! Friendly staff, affordable price and delicious. Recommended for sure!!!
