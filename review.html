Review

We are very recommended of this dessert cafe, Popis at Kuala Terengganu. The food just outstanding and they are good at what they are doing that making Popis in a variety of ways. The service is good, friendly at the counter and also during delivery. A simple popia wrap on goreng pisang is surprisingly addictive! Crispy poppy seeds, sweet bananas, chocolate and toppings are all delicious and premium. Perfect dessert!! Worth every cent.

Zurai

De White House Cafe is a cozy cafe with different space of seating. Their japanese chicken curry tonkatsu was surprisingly tasty. We tried the nasi lemak very special and tasty, it come with curry fish. We also ordered aglio smoked duck, it taste very delicious and will repeat again. The dessert, macaron and cake also very tasty. Pizza and unagi rice are just decent. We love the interior design of this cafe, and staff also very helpful and friendly. Fast service! 10/10.

I visited Kupinangmu cafe and I was impressed by the affordability and quality of the food. The cafe has a luxurious yet minimalist setting, making it a great place to hang out with friends. The portions at Kupinangmu cafe are generous, the chicken chop and fried rice are both tasty options. To wash it all down, I highly recommend trying the refreshing and unique Soda drink. They also have Rahmah's menu. Overall, Kupinangmu cafe is a great spot for affordable and tasty food, and I will definitely be visiting again.
